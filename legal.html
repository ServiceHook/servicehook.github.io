üîí Privacy Policy

Summary: We do not sell your data. We only collect what is necessary to provide the service.

1. Information We Collect

A. Account Data

When you sign up, we collect your email address and generate a unique User ID (UID). This is used solely for authentication and account recovery.

B. Link Data

We store the original URLs you submit, the custom aliases you create, and any optional passwords or expiry dates you configure.

C. Analytics Data

When a short link is clicked, we collect non-personally identifiable information to provide stats to the link creator:

  • IP Address: Used temporarily to determine rough geolocation (City/Country) and then discarded or hashed.
  • User Agent: Used to determine device type (Mobile/Desktop) and Browser.
  • Referrer: The website the user came from.

2. Third-Party Processors

We share data with specific vendors strictly for infrastructure purposes:

  • Google Firebase (USA): Database hosting and Authentication services.
  • Cloudflare (USA): DDoS protection, CDN, and Edge computing.
  • ApiFlash & QRServer: External APIs used to generate link preview screenshots and QR codes.
